Long Live the Web

Net neutrality and the the right to freely access the entirety of the web from any location is a fundamental principle of allowing the web to truly enhance human communication. Technology has become an extension to many people's social presence. With the advent of a pandemic, people rely on the web now more than ever before to remain connected. Geopolitical borders broadly limit the possible communication of any common person, and a neutral web resists the attempts of political entities to control information.
